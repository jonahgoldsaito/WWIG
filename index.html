WWIG.

What Would I Get (if i followed you)

Most of the stuff I want to see when I go to a Twitter timeline is drowned in a crap heap of things I don't want to see.

Often the window is filled with @Tweets that I wouldn't see anyway.

I just want to quickly glance and see what I'd get (WWIG) if I followed you. And why not a rough estimate of how many of those tweets I'd get per day.

WWIG is just a simple bookmarklet that filters through the tweets on a Twitter timeline and fades out the ones that wouldn't cross your stream. It also calculates some very rudimentary estimates of the user's tweets/day from the twitter API.

Simple. Just something I wished Twitter did natively.
