What Would I Get (If I Followed You)

WWIG filters through the tweets on a Twitter timeline and fades out the ones that wouldn't cross your stream.

It also calculates some very rudimentary stats (e.g. 3.9 Tweets / Day) right there with the native stats using the Twitter API.

WWIG

Just go to a user's timeline on twitter.com and click WWIG.

What do you get with WWIG?

  • All the @ tweets faded so you can see the real meat and potatoes
  • Tweets / Day stat (appended to the stats at top)
  • WWIG tweets / day stat (doesn't include the @ tweets)
  • A preview of what said-user would look like with a wig

Most of the stuff I want to see when I go to a Twitter timeline is drowned in a crap heap of things I don't want to see. A lot of those tweets will never grace my timeline.

I just want to quickly glance and see what I'd get (WWIG) if I followed you. The bookmarklet also adds some stats - a rough estimate of how many of your tweets I'd get per day. That seems pretty relevant.

Simple. Just something I wished Twitter did natively.
